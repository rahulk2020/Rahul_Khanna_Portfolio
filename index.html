About Me

Hi, My name is Rahul Khanna. I'm Currently live in Chandigarh. I completed my gradution in Bachlore's of Computer Application.
Welcome to my Portfolio! It’s a pleasure to have you here and an honor to introduce my work to you, so thank you for reading!
As I introduce my portfolio and you explore it for yourself if you want to, you’ll notice that I’ve worked with many design, exploring both print and web projects, and working in areas from branding to user interfaces to advertising and even more. I love how design can be used in so many different formats and mediums to portray similar content in various ways to provide a unique user experience, and I’m super interested in the way that print and web can work together in cool ways, so I’m always flicking between them and intertwining them together!
Within my portfolio, I also include work that has been created for various purposes, whether from university projects, commissioned freelance work, volunteer design or personal pieces of work, so you’ll see lots of different types of work!
