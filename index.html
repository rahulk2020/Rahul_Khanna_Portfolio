About Me

Hi, My name is Rahul Khanna. I currently live in Chandigarh. I completed my bachelor's of Computer Application.
I am a passionate Computer Teacher and IT professional with a strong foundation in computer fundamentals, programming, and digital literacy. Currently working at St. Mary’s School, Chandigarh, I focus on making technology engaging and easy to understand for students while fostering logical thinking and problem-solving skills.
With experience in teaching programming languages such as Python, Java, and JavaScript, along with tools like MS Office and modern classroom technologies, I strive to create interactive and practical learning environments. My role extends beyond teaching—I actively design lesson plans, manage computer labs, and support digital learning initiatives.
In addition to my teaching career, I have a creative edge as a graphic designer, working with tools like Canva, Photoshop, and Figma to produce impactful visuals and branding content. My diverse professional journey, including roles in recruitment, administration, and auditing, has helped me develop strong communication, organizational, and multitasking skills.
I am driven by a continuous desire to learn, grow, and contribute meaningfully—whether it’s in education, technology, or creative design.
